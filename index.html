Frequently asked questions

Discounts

After the end of the course, there are cumulative discounts: 10% for the second, 15% for the third and 25% for the fourth course. Discounts are valid for an unlimited period.

Certificate

Upon completion of the course, you will receive an electronic certificate. Students often use it when applying for a job, but it is worth remembering that the most valuable skill is the skill you learn during the course.

Work on a project and without it

If you have specific tasks and a project, you can work on them on the course under the supervision of a teacher. Not having your own project is not a problem. On the course, you get a profession by completing the assignments that we have prepared.

Employment

Students already on the course post information about themselves and contact customers, and teachers and curators give recommendations based on their specific situations.

Installment

You can get an interest-free installment plan for 3, 6, or 12 months.

Demand and relevance

All course programs are routinely updated every six months, or as major changes are released.
